01 — about me

Depth over surface.
Systems over shortcuts.

I'm Pari Jain, an IT student and AI/ML developer from India who genuinely wants to understand how things work — not just make them work. I prefer reading the paper over watching the tutorial, and building from scratch over calling the API.

My work spans deep learning, computer vision, NLP, audio classification, and full-stack AI applications. I'm drawn to technically difficult problems — the ones where the answer isn't obvious, and the solution demands first-principles thinking.

I contribute to open source through GSSoC, compete on Kaggle, and run experiments that are firmly research-adjacent. But I'm also a communicator — I've anchored 20+ events, led a college reader's club, and reported for the college media cell. Technical depth + human presence is a combination I've deliberately cultivated.
